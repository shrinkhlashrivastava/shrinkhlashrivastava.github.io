I am a UI/UX designer whose design principles are derived from the world's absence of empathy and is highly driven by insatiable curiosity in combination with the affection for resolving real user issues. At present, as a fresh intern, I am attempting to develop a design voice while working in UI/UX design through active contribution, relevant discussions, and passion-fueled work consisting of designing user interfaces that are more than just looks, including functionality and meaning. In my opinion, a good design reflects an unparalleled ability to appreciate the time and attention the users give.

I feel privileged to be working throughout the entire design process, beginning with research, wireframes, prototypes, and ending with the creation of high-fidelity prototypes because all these go into shaping purposeful products. It is not just the tools that assist me in creating my ideas, such as Figma, which fascinate me, but what happens outside of the screens most fascinates me.

As I keep learning more about myself, finding out about my design approach, I do know one thing for sure. What will anchor me is purposefully creating work that is collaborative, human-centric, and powered by the intention of dismantling the obstacles we are confronted with.
